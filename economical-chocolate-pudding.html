Economical Chocolate Pudding

serves 4-6

ingredients:

pudding

  • 75ml butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 ts baking powder
  • 2 Tbs cocoa
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 ts vanilla essence
  • 1/2 ts mixed spice
  • 1/2 ts cinnamon

sauce

  • 2 Tbs cocoa
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup hot water

instructions:

pudding

  1. cream butter, sugar and vanilla essence until fluffy.
  2. add egg and beat well.
  3. fold in sifted flour, mixed spice,cinnamon and cocoa with milk.
  4. pour mixture into oven-proof dish.

sauce

  1. mix the cocoa and sugar together then sprinkle ontop of the wet mixture.
  2. pour water (gently) on top using back of spoon to break the fall.

baking

  1. bake in a preheated oven at 180 deg C for 35-40 minutes.
  2. serve hot or cold.
