Chocolate Fridge Tart

serves 10

user1234

posted: 01 April

Comment Share +

ingredients:

  • 200g milk chocolate
  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 2 1/2 cups cream
  • 220g vanilla shortbread
  • 125g chocolate coated malt balls
  • 100g shelled pistachios
  • speckled eggs to decorate

instructions:

  1. line the bottom of a 20cm spring form cake tin with baking paper.
  2. chop both chocolates and place in a heat-safe bowl.
  3. double boil the chocolate until melted. stir until smooth.
  4. heat the cream in a sauce pan then stir into the melted chocolate.
  5. chop the short bread and pistachios into smaller pieces
  6. mix in the short bread, pistachios and malt chocolate and spoon into the tin. chill for 2 hours.
  7. to ease serving, wrap the sides of the tin in a warm cloth for about 3 mins to melt teh edges.
  8. lastly decorate with speckled eggs.
